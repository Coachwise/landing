  items:
    - q: "What is Coachwise?"
      a: "A training app for people who take it seriously. You log your sessions, a coach writes your plan, and your progress stays in one place instead of scattered across apps and notes. It's built for strength and climbing today, each with logging that fits the sport."
    - q: "Do I need a coach to use it?"
      a: "No. Plenty of people use Coachwise on their own to log and track their training. Whenever you want guidance, you pick a coach and take their package — and either way, your history stays yours."
    - q: "Is it just for lifting and climbing?"
      a: "Those are the two it's built around right now, each with logging that fits: barbell work with sets, reps and RPE, or climbing with grades, send type and hangboard hangs. If your week mixes both, it's still one training history."
    - q: "What language and calendar does it use?"
      a: "Persian and English, and both the Jalali and Gregorian calendars. Set whichever suits you and switch whenever you like — the whole app follows along."
    - q: "Is it on Android and iPhone?"
      a: "It's built for both. It isn't on Google Play or the App Store yet, so the download buttons here stay off until the listings are live — the moment they are, these buttons work."
    - q: "How does a coach get paid?"
      a: "A coach builds a package and sets its price. What an athlete pays lands in the coach's wallet, held safely until it clears, and the coach cashes out whenever they want."
    - q: "What's free, and what's paid?"
      a: "Getting started is free. Logging and saving your own training is part of Coachwise Pro. And when you take a coach's package, Pro comes with it for as long as the package runs — so a coached athlete never pays for it twice."
